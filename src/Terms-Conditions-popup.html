What is Meri Pasand®?

Meri Pasand® is a premier matchmaking and matrimonial portal that specialises in connecting individuals from the Pakistani and Indian subcontinent communities worldwide, as well as those seeking a life partner from these communities.

Meri Pasand® is the result of years of research into how to best fulfil the demands of a diverse range of age groups, changing cultural and community norms and expectations that come with living or growing up in different parts of the world, all within the framework of an evolving population.

In the conventional sense, we cater to the matrimonial needs of the elder generation, who are looking for a suitable spouse or wife for themselves, their sons or daughters, or other family members. At the same time, we appeal to the younger generation, which wants a partner they are in sync with—someone who shares their ideals, ethics, and perspectives.

Meri Pasand® is a safe place to make healthy and genuine connections on your path to finding your life companion. A comprehensive sign-up process ensures that all our members are genuinely looking for a life partner, and our verification process confirms their authenticity.

Meri Pasand® is an all-inclusive platform that allows people from all walks of life to find a life partner while remaining true to their personal, cultural, and religious beliefs.

Our search tools enable you to find a partner from your neighbourhood to anywhere in the world, while our personality test, developed by neuroscience experts, allows our algorithms to delve deeper for more accurate compatibility scores. Combined with our advanced filtering system, we are confident we can help you find that special someone you can boldly proclaim as "Meri Pasand."
